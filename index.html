We are the Scran and Scoff Club

Welcome: Feel at home with us!

This is the Scran and Scoff Club. If you match one of the following criteria, you are most welcome here:

  • Serving in the armed forces
  • A seafarer
  • Blue Light personnel
  • Have served
  • Someone who volunteers alongside us
  • A member of our host church's community
  • Someone from one of our symbiotic partner groups or organisations
  • Family member of someone who has served

Please feel at home with us. We were founded on Wednesday 8th January 2025 with one simple concept in mind - to provide a hearty meal, sit down together and eat and mutually support each other with any successes, failures and issues.

All our other activates have spun off from our meals together. For example, Forces of Nature gardening project.

Come: relax, enjoy good food, good company, vibes, good support and leave your rank behind when you sit down and eat with us!

Our Ethos

We believe this of ourselves: We, each one of us, have made a unique decision at least once in our lives to serve humanity in a very special way. Therefore, we fully understand each other's needs. We are therefore, indebted to our Principle Sponsor and to all our friends for strengthening our ability to support each other in profound, deeply meaningful and practical ways.

Our Principal Sponsor

The Parish of St Dunstan and All Saints', Stepney have opened their hearts and their doors to us. They provide the budget for us to buy the ingredients and then venue for free.
We are not a 'churchy' group. You will find a warm, discrete welcome from the church and leadership. What you won't find is 'Bible Bashing', proselytizing and judgement.
However, if you wish to tap into the church with a one to one chat with the rector or chaplain - that's on offer. If you wish to sit quietly in a corner of the church to pray - that's on offer. If you wish to talk about life events like baptism, confirmation or marriage - that's on offer. If you want to come to Mass - that's on offer. f you want to talk privately about spiritual matters - that's on offer. If you want to be left alone just to enjoy your lunch - that's on offer too!
